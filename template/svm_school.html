Welcome to SVM Higher Secondary School

SVM Higher Secondary School was established by Shri S'ad Vidya Mandal, a reputed trust in Bharuch. The school is affiliated with the Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board (GSEB) and offers education from Kindergarten to 12th grade.

Shri S’ad Vidya Mandal was established in 1954 as a public trust and is registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act 1950 and also under the Society’s Registration Act XXI of 1860. SVM Higher Secondary School was established by Shri S’ad Vidya Mandal, a reputed trust in Bharuch, which was established in 1954. Shri S’ad Vidya Mandal has established and manages several colleges with under & post graduate courses in Arts, Science, Commerce, Engineering, Computer Applications etc. SVM Higher Secondary School was established on 17th June 2009 and is an English medium co-educational institution. Affiliated to Gujarat State Secondary & Higher Secondary Education Board, Gujarat(GSHSEB). This school is managed by highly reputed Trust with well qualified & experienced teaching staff. Quality & Value- based education The academic syllabus is in total consonance with current trends Confidence & sound attitude building through co-curricular & extra –curricular activities Grooming in traditional as well as modern cosmopolitan Culture Individual attention to the children Special emphasis on cultivating the skill- sets to compete in all forms of examinations Regular seminars, talks, competitions, tours, medical camps, etc. Exposure to external exams like Olympiads, GK IQ, IAPT, & PRL Activity based learning in Pre-Primary section NCERT backed syllabus through regular Work Sheets in Lower and Upper Primary classes Well-maintained practical labs for Secondary & Higher Secondary sections Teaching through play- way method and multi- sensory approach for the Pre- primary children
